To show our thanks to the town of Diogenes for their generous support over the years, Ilion has opened up a small doggy daycare facility free of charge for residents! Ran by our very own (nanny dog), residents of Diogenes can bring their dogs here to socialize 5 days a week! Half a day (morning or afternoon) or full days are available. Unfortunately, overnight stays and boarding is unavailable at this time. All dogs must have updated shot records and check ups, which are also provided for free at the clinic.
