Emi is a normal girl from a military family. Along with her friends Jiayun and Taelun, she enlists in her nation's naval academy in order to protect her people against an ever-increasing pirate threat. When pirates attack and destroy the school, Emi and her crew are sent to the pirate homeland to defeat them for good.
