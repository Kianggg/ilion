CAFE

Have an appointment you're waiting for but you arrived too early? Or maybe you're looking to pet dogs as you sip a nice warm coffee? Then look no further and head on over to the Ilion cafe! With a variety of coffees and teas made with Alaskan brand beans and leaves, our baristas can whip up something soothing just for you! We also have a dessert menu full of delicious and sweet pastries that are unique to Ilion. Occasionally there will even be special menu items that will pop up! You can also find Ilion merchandise inside of the cafe too~ From plushies, postcards, buttons, t-shirts, and more! Show your support by finding merch of your favorite dog!
